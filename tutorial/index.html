Introduction

Open the application at: http://localhost:8080.

The Lift framework uses a view-first pattern. Lets look at how this is utilized to produce the content pages.

From /src/main/webapp/index.html notice the first line: 

<lift:surround with="default" at="content">
This is telling Lift to wrap itself with the /src/main/webapp/templates-hidden/default.html template at the content tag: 
<lift:bind name="content"></lift:bind>
